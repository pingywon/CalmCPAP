Getting started

Up and running in minutes

No software, no cloud setup. Just your SD card, a ZIP, and a browser.

1

Copy your SD card

Power off your CPAP, remove the SD card, and copy the entire card contents to your computer.

2

Create a ZIP archive

Zip the copied folder root. Trim older DATALOG folders if you want a smaller file.

3

Download CPAP

Grab the single index.html from GitHub releases. That's the entire app.

4

Open & explore

Open index.html in any browser, load your ZIP, pick a night, and start exploring your charts.
