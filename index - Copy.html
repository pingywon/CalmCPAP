Read this first

    
Sleep and CPAP therapy are serious. CalmCPAP is not.

    
CalmCPAP is a local browser viewer for curiosity, education, and entertainment. It can help you look around your export data, but it can also be incomplete, wrong, oversimplified, or misleading.

    

      

        
What this viewer is

        
    
          
  • A local browser app for opening CPAP SD-card ZIP exports.
    • 
          
  • A way to inspect nights, charts, and machine-reported trends for personal interest.
    • 
          
  • Something to treat as a rough viewer, not as a clinical report.
    • 
        

      

      

        
What this viewer is not

        
    
          
  • Not medical advice, diagnosis, titration guidance, or pressure-setting advice.
    • 
          
  • Not a substitute for your sleep clinician, respiratory therapist, or equipment provider.
    • 
          
  • Not a tool for deciding whether to change or stop CPAP therapy.
    • 
        

      

      

        Do not change CPAP settings, stop therapy, dismiss symptoms, or judge treatment success based on this app alone. If you have breathing trouble, oxygen concerns, severe daytime sleepiness, chest pain, or other urgent symptoms, contact a qualified clinician or seek emergency care instead of relying on this viewer.
