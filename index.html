Ten Views

Leak, pressure, flow, snore, respiratory rate, tidal volume, EPR, events, compare, and a plain-language overview.

How it works

From SD card to readable chart in four steps.

The app expects a ZIP made from the root of a CPAP SD-card copy, keeping the full folder structure intact so it can find what it needs.

Remove the SD card

Power off your CPAP and safely eject the SD card.

Copy the contents

Drag the full SD contents into a folder on your computer.

Zip the folder

Create one .zip. Optional: trim old DATALOG/ days to shrink it.

Open in the app

Drop the ZIP in, pick a night, and the charts render locally.

Signals

Ten plots from the same ZIP you already have.

No manual setup. The app inventories what's in the archive and lights up the tabs it can plot.

Overview
Leak and pressure together for a plain-language read of the night.
Leak
Mask-seal behavior and time above the large-leak guide line.
Pressure
Therapy pressure across the detected session.
Flow
Breathing waveform, shape changes, and pauses.
Snore
Vibration-based snore activity signal.
Respiratory Rate
Breaths per minute over time.
Tidal Volume
Air moved per breath, with male/female baseline references.
EPR
Therapy vs exhalation pressure, with derived relief amount.
Events
Parsed annotations, deduplicated per code, with a full glossary.
Compare
Overlay several signals on one plot, normalized or raw.
Privacy & Scope

No upload. No account. No cloud.

This is a single local file. Your data is read into your browser and never sent anywhere.

What stays where

  • Runs locally in your browser. No data leaves your computer.
  • No backend, no account, no telemetry. There's nothing to sign up for.
  • Tested on ResMed AirSense 11 SD exports. Other devices may load partially or not at all.
  • Ships as one self-contained file so you can audit, archive, or open it offline.
Not medical advice. Intended for exploration and curiosity — not diagnosis or treatment. Don't rely on it for treatment decisions. If something looks off, talk to your doctor or sleep specialist.
